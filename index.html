Gatwick Drop Off Charge

Everything London drivers need to know about Gatwick drop off charges, payments, free alternatives, and pick up rules for smooth journeys.

What is the Gatwick Drop Off Charge?

The Gatwick Airport drop off charge applies at both the North and South Terminal forecourts. It was introduced to reduce congestion, manage traffic and support Gatwick’s sustainability goals.

How Much Does It Cost?

The Gatwick drop off charges are structured to encourage quick drop offs:

0–10 minutes

£6

After 10 minutes

£1/min

30–60 minutes

£25

Over 60 minutes

£150

How to Pay the Gatwick Drop Off Charge

Payments must be made online by midnight the following day. You can pay Gatwick drop off with a debit or credit card, or set up auto-pay if you visit often.

Forgot to Pay?

If you forgot to pay Gatwick drop off charge , you may receive a £100 PCN (reduced to £60 if paid within 14 days). ANPR cameras record all vehicles automatically.

Free Drop Off Options

You can avoid charges by using the Long Stay Car Parks, which offer up to 2 hours of free parking. Shuttle buses take passengers to terminals quickly and conveniently book your Airport Taxi

What About Pick Up?

Gatwick pick up charges apply in short stay car parks near each terminal. Fees start at around £5 for 30 minutes. Picking up is not allowed at forecourt drop off zones.

Gatwick South Drop Off Payment

The South Terminal drop off payment follows the same rules as the North. Number plates are captured on entry, and payment must be made online by midnight the next day.

Why the Charge Exists

  • Traffic management: Reduce forecourt congestion at a busy airport.
  • Environmental goals: Discourage unnecessary drop offs to cut emissions.
  • Encourage alternatives: Promote taxis, trains, and long-stay parking.

Tips for Drivers

  • Bookmark the online Gatwick drop off payment site or register for auto-pay.
  • Use long stay car parks for free drop offs if time allows.
  • Don’t overstay – extra minutes add up quickly.
  • Always use short stay car parks for pickups.

Final Thoughts

The Gatwick drop off charge is here to stay, and being informed helps London locals avoid stress and fines. Plan ahead, pay online, or choose free alternatives for smoother journeys.
